Fold your mini zine

One letter sheet · eight panels · no staples.

  1. Print the sheet landscape at 100% scale, margins set to none.
  2. Fold in half lengthwise (long edge to long edge), then unfold.
  3. Fold in half widthwise, then fold each half back to the middle — you now have eight panels.
  4. Unfold to the lengthwise half-fold. Cut along the center crease between the two middle panels only.
  5. Open flat, fold lengthwise again, push the two ends toward the middle — the slit opens into a diamond.
  6. Keep pushing until the panels collapse into a booklet, then crease with the front cover facing out.

Panel numbers are printed faintly in the corner of each panel on screen preview only — they are not exported.
